Tech

The MediLOT Technologies

MediLOT is built on the principles of patient centricity, privacy, and equitable data sharing. For truly personalised medicine, large datasets need to be analysed and interpreted. The MediLOT health data protocol enables this via several innovative and proprietary technologies.

Our Innovation

GEMINI

GEMINI is a GEneralizable Medical Information aNalysis and Integration platform. The objective is to design and implement an integrative healthcare analytics system to address various kinds of healthcare problems. The systems integrated in GEMINI cover data acquisition, data cleaning, data integration, data processing, data analytics and data visualization steps of the big data analytics pipeline.

ForkBase

ForkBase is a system that supports high-level properties demanded in many modern applications, including data versioning, collaboration and security. With its versatile programming interface, rich semantics and high performance, ForkBase enables rapid developments of many classes of scalable, distributed applications.

Read more »

 

Hyperledger++

Hyperledger++ is an enhanced blockchain infrastructure of Hyperledger. It uses trusted hardware to improve trust relation between nodes and its throughput by a wide margin. The design of Hyperledger++ follows two novel but critical principles, namely scale up by scaling down and prioritize consensus messages, that enable the blockchain consensus protocols to scale.

  Provision of Healthcare AI and analytics

Healthcare AI is powerful but harder to build than thought to be, especially when data appear to be the main barrier. So is for healthcare analytics that aims to discovery insights from tons of patient data. On the one hand, technical complexity is inherent in constructing healthcare AI and analytics. On the other hand, patient data are sensitive and hence should be always protected from any unauthorized use. Our AI suite, GEMINI, is developed to ease the construction of healthcare AI and analytics, in a practical manner while strictly preserving patients' privacy.

MediLOT Technologies provide comprehensive support for developing AI/analytics applications on top of GEMINI as well as running the applications effecting on real patient data in the GEMINI sandbox. Our past success cases include:

  • Food journaling for prediabetes prevention (the Foodlg app)
  • Patient readmission prediction
  • Disease progression modelling
  • MRI/X-ray analytics

  Architecting Blockchain Service for Regional Health System

Today, patient EHR history is inevitably fragmented across health agencies she/he ever visited. By looking into the issue, trustiness is the main obstacle of inter-agency EMR data sharing. Such situation definitely needs to be changed towards building a patient-centric health system. The emerging blockchain technology can be relied on for the revolution, and MediLOT Technologies are specialized at architecting the healthcare blockchain ecosystem. To that end, MediLOT Technologies provide the following services:

  • Building inter-operational blockchain across health agencies
  • Protocol design for both on-chain and off-chain data manipulation
  • Privacy-preserving data access control and authorization

  Profiling Blockchain Implementation for Healthcare DApp

As emerging blockchain implementations are diverse and complex, no industrial standard has yet been established for profiling their performance breakdown. Our research product BlockBench is the first-ever benchmark framework for permissioned/private blockchain systems such as Hyperledger Fabric, Ethereum and Parity. Details of BlockBench are explained in our research paper.

BlockBench is open source and free of use. Apart from that, MediLOT Technologies provide consultation for customized benchmarking as well as certifying benchmark report, specially for blockchain adoption in healthcare DApp. In particular, we provide the following services:

  • Providing smart contract specification and utilities for customized benchmarking.
  • Supervising customized benchmark and certifying reports.
  • Consultation for analyzing performance breakdown as well as bottleneck analysis.

  Storage Support for Multi-tenant Healthcare Analytics

Data is the base of any kind of analytics. Healthcare data such as EHR can usually be used for multiple purposes of analytics, which form the collaboration of healthcare analytics among doctors, medical researchers, health agencies and institutes, etc. Collaborative analytics requires different formats of the data produced by intricate processing such as cleaning, transformation and integration. This essentially requires database to store versions of the data, which not only introduces massive data redundancy as each data version is stored as a stand-alone copy, but also renders data versions hard to govern.

ForkBase is a collaboration-centric storage system that fully supports collaborative analytics. Unlike traditional database being data-centric only, ForkBase facilitates data forking, version management and access control to the end of multi-tenant analytics. Moreover, ForkBase is endowed with a novel data de-duplication technology that can remarkably reduce data redundancy among data versions in the physical storage. Technical details are explained in our research paper. MediLOT Technologies provide consultation for the deployment of ForkBase and technical support for client application integration.
